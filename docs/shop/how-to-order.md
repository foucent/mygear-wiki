To provide solid pre-sales advice—and because of payment fees and technical costs—I currently only support ordering via **WhatsApp**. Send your item list and shipping address, confirm the quote, pay by Wise (or bank transfer).

## Purchase steps

### 1. Message me with what you need

On WhatsApp, send the exact models and options, plus your **country / city or postal code** so I can calculate shipping.

Example:

```text
Please quote (ship to Los Angeles, USA):
- 1× Butterfly Zyre 03 2.7mm Black
- 1× DHS H3 Neo National Blue 40° 2.1mm Black
Need assembly? Yes / No
```

<div class="admonition tip">
  <p class="admonition-title">Tip</p>
  <p>If anything is out of stock or restricted by region/brand, I tell you in advance and help adjust the list.</p>
</div>

### 2. Confirm the order & pay

After I confirm your requirements, send me your shipping address on WhatsApp. Example (not a real address):

```text
123 Main St, Los Angeles, CA 90012, USA
```

I then prepare a formal **order form / spreadsheet** for a second confirmation. Once you confirm it, pay by:

- **Recommended:** [Wise](https://wise.com/invite/ahpc/pingl621) / Alipay (Southeast Asia customers) — usually instant
- **Bank transfer** (1–3 business days) — fees may be high in some countries (e.g. Canada)
- **PayPal** (not recommended) — **5%** extra fee

After you pay, send a quick “Paid” note — I confirm when the payment is received.

<div class="admonition tip">
  <p class="admonition-title">Tip</p>
  <p>Register on <a href="https://wise.com/invite/ahpc/pingl621">Wise</a> in advance; first-time verification can take time.</p>
</div>

### 3. Shipping & tracking

- In-stock items are processed after payment (weekend orders often start on Monday).
- You get a **tracking number** after the parcel is on the plane.

<div class="admonition tip">
  <p class="admonition-title">Tip</p>
  <p>For high-value or heavy orders, I can <strong>split into 2+ parcels</strong> on request (safer if one package is delayed).</p>
</div>

Packing photos and trackers: [Shipping & Delivery](shipping-and-delivery.md).

## Payment & order changes

After payment, I start sourcing and packing. If an item becomes unavailable, I contact you to swap, cancel that line, or keep the balance as credit toward your next order (full remittance out of China can be restricted by FX rules — credit / partial adjustment is usually smoother).

You can still adjust contents before packing is finalized — message me as early as possible.

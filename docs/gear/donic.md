# Donic

Brand gear spotlights. Each model opens with a hero shot; tap any photo to enlarge.

## Blades

### Zhang Jike Original Carbon

Chinese players often call this **红张** (“Red Zhang”). Classic **outer 5+2** Viscaria-style build with hard face and fiber near the surface—fast near/mid-table offense, usually seen in **FL**. Polarizing feel: direct pop and pressure for compact, active strokes; less ideal if you want soft dwell. Live USD reference: [Pricing & Sourcing](../shop/pricing-and-sourcing.md).

**Anders Lind Hexa Carbon** (**FL**)—Hexamid carbon **5+2**, Denmark-flag handle cosmetics. Less common than the big Donic signature blanks; a clean fiber option in the Lind line.
